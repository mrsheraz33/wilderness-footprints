8 DAYS HONEYMOON SAFARIS

Lake Manyara · Ngorongoro Crater · Serengeti National Park

TANZANIA HONEYMOONS – WHY IS TANZANIA SO GOOD

Tanzania is superb as a honeymoon destination, not only for its stunning natural landscapes and wildlife, but also because you can take a safari and beach within such close proximity of each other. With such efficient internal flights to whisk you from luxury safari to luxury beach, the memories you make in Tanzania will be with you forever. And of course, the climate is excellent in all months other than April and May, specifically during our summer and winter wedding/honeymoon seasons.

DAY 1: ARRIVAL DAY

Arriving in Kilimanjaro International Airport from home and meet our representative. After greetings you will be transferred to the hotel in Moshi for hot shower and overnight at a designated hotel.

DAY 2: ARUSHA TOWN – LAKE MANYARA NATIONAL PARK

After breakfast you are met by our tour guide/driver, who will deliver a short safari briefing. You will be driving to Lake Manyara National Park for a game drive with our picnic lunch. Upon your arrival Manyara you will be welcomed by the Great Rift Valley Escarpment view and fun makers' baboon at the entrance of Manyara in large groups. We will travel through the agricultural and fresh produce market town of Mto Wa Mbu, which is situated next to the entrance of the Lake Manyara National Park.

The Lake Manyara National Park is one of Tanzania's most dramatically located African wildlife areas, consisting of a massive but shallow soda lake at the foot of the Great Rift Valley's western escarpment. The park's varied habitat attracts a wide variety of animals; including one of Africa's densely populated of herd of African elephant, famous tree-climbing lions, as well as large flocks of flamingos. Late afternoon you will be transferred to Lake Manyara Hotel or Lake Manyara Serena Lodge for hot shower, dinner and overnight.

DAY 3: SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

After breakfast depart for Serengeti National Park passing through Olduvai Gorge, the site of Leakey's family discovery of fossils of human and animal ancestors dating back to almost two million years. You will have your picnic lunch on the way to Serengeti. Upon your arrival you will check in to the lodge for hot shower, dinner and overnight at Serengeti Sopa / Seronera Wildlife Lodge or Serengeti Serena.

DAY 4 – 5: SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK – FULL DAY

In the morning after breakfast you will meet your tour guide for starting a game drive to Serengeti National Park for a full day game drive. Serengeti is the largest national park in Tanzania covering more than 14,000 square kilometers. The park is having numerous numbers of unique features and attractions among them include: The annual great wildebeest migration, the park is famous for its endless plains and the savannah vegetation that it owns, balloon safari and been the richest park full of wildlife and birdlife elsewhere in Africa.

The park also supports many species of wildlife including Lion, African Leopard, African Elephant, cheetah, African Buffalo, Thomson's and Grant's gazelle, topi, eland, water buck, hyena, baboon, impala, African wild dog and giraffe. The park also boasts about 500 bird species. Your tour guide will therefore take you back late in the afternoon at your lodge for hot shower, dinner and overnight.

DAY 6: NGORONGORO CRATER

In the morning after breakfast you will meet your driver guide and been transfer to Ngorongoro crater gate with your picnic lunch ready for a full day crater tour, explore the crater which is famous for its great concentration of wildlife and bird life. If you're lucky enough you can see a predator hunting a prey. Ngorongoro crater is famous for its different attraction which includes alkaline lakes, forest, different vegetation, hot springs, different kinds of wildlife and birdlife and its landscape. In the late afternoon you will ascend the crater floor and been transferred to Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge / Ngorongoro Serena for hot shower, dinner and overnight.

DAY 7: LAKE MANYARA NATURE WALK

Leave after breakfast for nature walk Mto Wa Mbu Village lunch in the middle of banana farm later walk back to the lodge for hot shower, dinner and overnight at Lake Manyara Hotel or Lake Manyara Serena lodge.

DAY 8: ARUSHA / MOSHI / DEPARTURE

After breakfast visit of a Masai Village, then drive back to Arusha. We will arrive in Arusha in the afternoon for shopping and other formalities or relaxing and resting in a day room. You can decide to extend your trip to Zanzibar beach holiday. Else we will transfer you to the Kilimanjaro International Airport ready for flight back home.
