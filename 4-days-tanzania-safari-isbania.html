4 DAYS TANZANIA SAFARI

From Isebania • Serengeti • Ngorongoro • Tarangire

4 Days Tour

4 days Tanzania tour from Isebania is a Tanzania wildlife safari which will cover Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater and Tarangire National Park.

Note: Accommodations offered in this category, are medium priced small tented lodges/camps with private bathroom facilities. These are small in size which assures personalized services and excellent ratio between quality and price.

Also: You can do the same package on the public budget camping safari, REQUEST PRICES

4 days Tanzania tour from Isebania will take you through: Isebania Border → Serengeti National Park → Ngorongoro Crater → Tarangire National Park → Arusha

1

Isebania Border – Serengeti National Park

Lunch, Dinner

Your safari will begin with pick up from Isebania border where you will be met by your guide in Tanzania. You will proceed to your lodge in Serengeti national park for check in and dinner and overnight stay at Serengeti heritage luxury tented camp OR Similar.

2

Full Day in Serengeti National Park

Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

After a morning breakfast at your lodge, you will explore Serengeti national park during a morning and afternoon game drive as you search for wildlife species such as elephants, wildebeests, hippos, giraffes, zebras, gazelles, impalas, kudu, cheetahs, lions, leopards among others. Return to your lodge for dinner and overnight.

3

Ngorongoro Crater Tour

Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

After Early Morning Breakfast, leave Serengeti to the Ngorongoro crater. Descend about 600 meters into the crater to search for wildlife like lions, leopards, elephants, buffalos, rhinos, hyenas, zebras, wildebeests. See Lake Magadi with flamingos. Overnight at Karatu Country lodge OR Similar.

4

Tarangire National Park – Arusha

Breakfast, Lunch

Drive to Tarangire National Park known for large herds of elephants and ancient baobab trees. Enjoy afternoon game drive searching for gazelles, impalas, zebras, waterbucks. After Picnic Lunch, transfer to Arusha where services end or transfer to Airport for home flight.

WHAT IS INCLUDED

  • 4×4 safari land cruiser
  • Accommodation per itinerary
  • Meals as per itinerary (B, L, D)
  • Services of literate English driver/guide
  • National park entrance fees
  • Concession and crater fees
  • Excursions & activities as per itinerary
  • Drinking water in the safari vehicle

WHAT IS EXCLUDED

  • International Flights
  • Visas and related costs
  • Personal expenses (drinks, laundry, telephone)
  • Optional excursions not listed
  • Covid-19 related tests
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Anything else not included in itinerary
