CYCLING TOUR

Kilimanjaro & Moshi Cycling Tours

Experience Tanzania from a whole new perspective with our exhilarating cycling tours around the iconic Mount Kilimanjaro and the lush landscapes of Moshi. Perfect for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and cycling enthusiasts.

1. West Kilimanjaro Cycling

Scenic Route

Discover the hidden gems of West Kilimanjaro on this scenic cycling route. Ride through local villages, coffee plantations, and forested paths, all while enjoying spectacular views of Kilimanjaro's western slopes. Immerse yourself in local culture and nature on this unforgettable ride.

2. Kilimanjaro Cycling – 6 Days Marangu Route via Kilema Road

Multi-Day Challenge

Embark on a six-day cycling adventure following the famous Marangu Route via Kilema Road. This route combines the thrill of cycling with breathtaking scenery: lush forests, volcanic landscapes, and panoramic views of Kilimanjaro's snow-capped peaks. Ideal for cyclists looking for a multi-day challenge with cultural stops along the way.

Highlights:

Ride through Kilema Road and surrounding trails
Scenic views of Mount Kilimanjaro from different angles
Visits to local villages and cultural experiences
Camping or lodge stays along the route

3. Moshi Cycling Tour – TPC Plantation

Half/Full Day

Perfect for a half-day or full-day cycling excursion, this tour takes you around Moshi town and the TPC coffee plantations. Enjoy gentle rides through scenic farmland, interact with local communities, and witness the coffee-making process from bean to cup.

Highlights:

Easy to moderate cycling for all levels
Coffee plantation tour with tastings
Picturesque views of Kilimanjaro in the background
Ideal for families, casual riders, and photography enthusiasts
