KILI TREKS

Mount Kilimanjaro Trekking Routes & Prices

Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro (5,895m) is a once-in-a-lifetime adventure. There are 7 main trekking routes, each offering a different experience in terms of scenery, difficulty, success rate, and comfort. Choosing the right route is key to a successful summit.

Kilimanjaro Routes Overview

There are 7 official routes: Lemosho, Machame, Marangu, Rongai, Umbwe, Shira, and Northern Circuit

1. Lemosho Route

$2,200 - $3,200
ModerateSuccess: 90%+ Camping 7-9 days

Best Overall - Most scenic route, excellent acclimatization

Highlights: Rainforest, Shira Plateau, panoramic views

Best for: First-time climbers

2. Machame Route

$1,800 - $2,800
Moderate-Challenging Success: 80-85% Camping 6-7 days

Most Popular - Very scenic, good acclimatization

Highlights: "Climb high, sleep low"

Best for: Adventurous trekkers

3. Marangu Route

$1,400 - $2,000
ModerateSuccess: 65-75% Huts 5-6 days

Comfort Route - Budget-friendly, hut accommodation

Best for: Budget travelers & beginners

4. Rongai Route

$1,900 - $2,700
ModerateSuccess: 80-85% Camping 6-7 days

Quiet Route - Only route from north, less crowded

Best for: Those seeking a quiet experience

5. Northern Circuit

$2,800 - $3,800
ModerateSuccess: 90%+ Camping 8-10 days

Best Success Rate - Longest route, best acclimatization

Best for: High success & premium experience

6. Umbwe Route

$1,700 - $2,500
Very DifficultSuccess: ~60% Camping 5-6 days

Most Challenging - Fast, steep, scenic

Best for: Experienced climbers only

7. Shira Route

$2,000 - $2,800
ModerateSuccess: ~85% Camping 6-7 days

Similar to Lemosho - Starts at higher altitude

Not ideal for beginners due to altitude start

Mount Kilimanjaro Trekking

Umbwe & Rongai Route Circuit

$2,350

Experience the ultimate Mount Kilimanjaro adventure by combining two of the mountain's most iconic routes — the challenging Umbwe Route and the scenic Rongai Route Circuit. This unforgettable trekking experience offers dramatic landscapes, remote wilderness, excellent acclimatization, and breathtaking summit views from Africa's highest peak. Starting through the steep and adventurous Umbwe Route, trekkers pass dense rainforest, giant heathers, alpine desert, and glacier landscapes before reaching Uhuru Peak at 5,895 meters. The journey continues through the peaceful northern side of Kilimanjaro via the Rongai Route Circuit, known for its quieter trails and stunning panoramic scenery.

This route combination is perfect for adventurous climbers seeking a unique Kilimanjaro trekking experience with diverse landscapes, fewer crowds, and rewarding summit success.

Package Information

Both our Standard and All-Inclusive Packages include: 2 Nights Hotel prior to the climb + 1 Night at Hotel post climb. Total trip takes 12 days (not including flights).

1

DAY 1: RAINFOREST ZONE

2,100m → 2,821m

Pick up from hotel. Drive to Lemosho Entrance Gate (3hrs). Trek through lush rainforest. You may see Colobus Monkeys. Trek: 2-3 hrs.

2

DAY 2: HEATHER / MOORLAND ZONE

2,821m → 3,444m

Climb to Shira 1 Camp. Trek: 5-7 hrs.

3

DAY 3: HEATHER / MOORLAND ZONE

3,444m → 4,200m

Climb to Moir Camp. Trek: 5-7 hrs.

4

DAY 4: LAVA TOWER

4,200m → 4,600m

Climb to Lava Tower Camp. Optional: climb Lava Tower. Trek: 3 hrs.

5

DAY 5: ACCLIMATIZATION HIKE

4,600m → 4,900m

Acclimatization hike to Arrow Glacier Camp (2-3 hrs).

6

DAY 6: ARROW GLACIER CAMP

4,600m → 4,900m

Climb to Arrow Glacier Camp. Trek: 1-1.5 hrs.

7

DAY 7: WESTERN BREACH

4,900m → 5,730m

Cross Western-Breach to Crater Camp. Trek: 5-7 hrs.

Two Options Available

Option #1 - Summit Day 7

Summit in mid-afternoon with very few climbers.

Option #2 - Summit Day 8

Traditional sunrise summit experience.

8

DAY 8: SUMMIT DAY

Option #1: Descend to Mweka Camp.
Option #2: Ascent to Uhuru Peak, then descend to Mweka Camp.

9

DAY 9: MWEKA GATE & OFFICIAL CERTIFICATE

Descend to Mweka Gate, receive Official Kilimanjaro Certificate. Drive back to Arusha.
