Tanzania Safari Circuits

March 27, 2026 Travel With Expert in Tanzania

Northern Circuit Safari

The Northern Circuit is the most popular safari route in Tanzania, offering world-famous parks, excellent road access, and diverse wildlife experiences.

Key Destinations

  • Serengeti National Park – Great Migration
  • Ngorongoro Crater – UNESCO Site
  • Tarangire National Park – Elephants & Baobabs
  • Lake Manyara – Tree-climbing lions
  • Arusha National Park – Short safaris

Highlights

  • Witness the Great Migration
  • Big Five game viewing
  • Savannah, crater, forest, lakes
  • Easy access from Arusha
Recommended: 5-8 days

Best For: First-time visitors, classic safari experiences

Southern Circuit Safari

The Southern Circuit is perfect for travelers seeking a quieter, more exclusive safari away from the crowds.

Key Destinations

  • Nyerere National Park (Selous) – Largest protected area
  • Ruaha National Park – Large predators
  • Mikumi National Park – Open plains

Highlights

  • Boat safaris on Rufiji River
  • Walking safaris with guides
  • Fewer crowds, exclusive feel
Recommended: 4-7 days

Best For: Repeat visitors, nature lovers, off-the-beaten-path

Western Circuit Safari

The Western Circuit is the most remote and untouched safari region, offering rare wildlife encounters and breathtaking scenery.

Key Destinations

  • Mahale Mountains – Chimpanzee trekking
  • Gombe Stream – Jane Goodall's chimps
  • Katavi National Park – Buffalo & hippos

Highlights

  • Chimpanzee trekking experience
  • Remote untouched wilderness
  • Views of Lake Tanganyika
Recommended: 6-10 days

Best For: Adventure travelers, wildlife enthusiasts

Popular Day-Trip Safaris – Northern Circuit

Tarangire National Park Day Safari

Famous for huge elephant herds and baobab trees. Wildlife includes lions, leopards, giraffes, zebras.

Lake Manyara National Park Day Safari

Known for tree-climbing lions and flamingos. Great for landscape and wildlife photography.

Ngorongoro Crater Day Safari

High density of animals including lions, elephants, rhinos. Full-day private safari experience.

Arusha National Park Day Trip

Mix of wildlife, scenic landscapes, optional canoeing on Momella Lakes. Ideal for beginners.

Tips for Day Trips

Most safaris include hotel pickup/drop-off, 4×4 safari vehicle with professional driver-guide, park entrance fees, picnic lunch, and bottled water. Early morning departures recommended for maximum wildlife viewing.
