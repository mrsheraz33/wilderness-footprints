MOUNT OLDONYO LENGAI

"Mountain of God" • The Only Active Carbonatite Volcano on Earth

About Oldonyo Lengai

Oldonyo Lengai, often referred to as the "Mountain of God" by the Maasai people, is an active stratovolcano located in Tanzania. It is situated in the East African Rift and is notable for being the only volcano on Earth that erupts carbonatite lava, which has a very low viscosity compared to typical basaltic lava.

Height

2,878m (9,200 ft)

Location

Near Lake Natron, Tanzania

Activity

Significance
