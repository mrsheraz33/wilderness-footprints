Discover Tanzania in Safari

DISCOVER TANZANIA IN SAFARI

Welcome to WILDERNESS FOOTPRINTS SAFARIS,

Our company has a team of experienced, caring and dedicated guides. All of them are native and therefore knows how to guide travelers to the natural beauty and wildlife of their native country. They have taken naturalist courses and are excellent game spotters.

All our vehicles are custom-made for safaris to withstand the harsh terrain while offering game-viewing comfort. In addition they are fitted with HF radio communications and all passengers are a guaranteed window seats.

Safaris in Tanzania is our passion so if you have been contemplating on doing an African Safari, then this is the right direction you have taken. We offer the most intriguing and awe-inspiring safari tours to Tanzania. "WILDERNESS FOOTPRINTS SAFARI" stand tall and supreme amongst all Safaris in Africa owing to what this great Country has to offer in terms of wildlife safaris. This is wrapped up with an unmatched cultural heritage, hospitality, and peace that prevail in this wonderful country. No one else can arrange great safaris in Tanzania in the most professional and In details manner than WILDERNESS FOOTPRINTS SAFARI

Our Mission Is To

To provide up to date and comprehensive information on Safaris so that your experience is as fulfilling and safe as possible. To promote responsible tourism to Tanzania so that the natural environment and local communities that depend on safari are positively impacted by your visit

To promote safe and responsible safari experiences in Tanzania, with a particular emphasis on ensuring Cooks and guides are treated fairly and ethically.
