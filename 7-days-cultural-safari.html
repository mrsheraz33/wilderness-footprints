7 DAYS CULTURAL SAFARI

Tarangire · Lake Manyara · Lake Eyasi · Serengeti · Ngorongoro

1 Arusha Arrival

Picked up from the airport and transferred to your lodge for overnight.

2 Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, go to Tarangire National Park for game drives, evening leave the park and go for overnight at your lodge.

3 Lake Manyara National Park + Mto wa Mbu

Leave in the morning to Lake Manyara National Park for game drives, in the afternoon leave the park and stop at Mto wa Mbu for a cultural tour. Evening go overnight at your lodge in Karatu area.

4 Lake Eyasi - Hadzabe & Datoga Tribes

Leave so early in the morning to Lake Eyasi area to catch and join an early morning local hunting activities with the Hadzabe. You will go to hunt with them the small animals, birds etc. Then in the same area, visit another tribe, Datoga, to see how they perform their blacksmith duties. Both tribes using the local methods applied it the Stone Age era. Later in the afternoon you will go back to Karatu for overnight at the same lodge.

5 Serengeti National Park

Leave in the morning, go to Serengeti National Park for game drives, evening go to overnight at your lodge in Serengeti.

6 Full Day Serengeti

Another game drives in Serengeti to explore more about this beautiful park.

7 Ngorongoro Highlands

Leave in the morning to Ngorongoro highlands, upon arrival descend into the Crater floor for a half day Crater tour. Evening ascend and go to your lodge for overnight.

8 Back to Arusha

Leave after breakfast, have hot lunch in Arusha then proceed to Airport to connect with your International flight back home.
