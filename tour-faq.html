What is the best time to visit Tanzania?

Tanzania can be visited year-round, but the best time depends on your interests:

  • Wildlife Safaris: June to October (dry season)
  • Mount Kilimanjaro Trekking: January to March and June to October
  • Beach Holidays (Zanzibar & Pemba): June to October and December to February
2

Do I need a visa to visit Tanzania?

Yes, most travelers require a visa. You can obtain a Tanzania eVisa online before arrival or get a visa on arrival at major entry points. Check your country's requirements before travel.

3

What vaccinations or health precautions are required?

  • Yellow fever vaccination required from risk countries
  • Malaria prevention strongly recommended
  • Hepatitis A, Typhoid, and Tetanus advised
  • Consult your doctor before traveling
4

What currency is used in Tanzania?

The official currency is Tanzanian Shilling (TZS). US dollars are widely accepted, especially in safari lodges and tourist areas. Credit cards accepted in major hotels, but cash preferred in remote locations.

5

What languages are spoken?

Swahili is the national language. English is widely spoken, especially in hotels, safari lodges, and tourist areas.

6

Do you provide customized tour itineraries?

Yes! We can tailor your Tanzania holiday according to your preferences, whether it's safari, trekking, beach, or cultural experiences. Just request your personalized itinerary.

7

Are the tours suitable for families and children?

Absolutely! We offer family-friendly safaris and cultural tours, with activities that are safe and enjoyable for children of all ages.

8

What should I pack for my Tanzania holiday?

  • Lightweight clothing for day, warm clothes for evenings
  • Comfortable walking shoes or hiking boots
  • Hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, insect repellent
  • Camera / binoculars for wildlife spotting
9

Is it safe to travel in Tanzania?

Tanzania is generally safe for tourists. Avoid walking alone at night in urban areas, follow your guide's instructions during safaris and treks, and keep valuables secure.

10

How can I book a Tanzania holiday tour?

You can contact us directly, send an inquiry, or request a personalized itinerary. We offer flexible packages including safari, trekking, and beach tours.
