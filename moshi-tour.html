MOSHI TOUR

Gateway to Adventure • Explore the Best of Moshi

Moshi, located at the foothills of Mount Kilimanjaro, is the perfect base for unforgettable adventures. From scenic hikes to cultural experiences, we offer a variety of tours that showcase the best of this vibrant region.

Mount Kilimanjaro Day Tours

Machame Gate Day Hike

Experience breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro's lower slopes on a guided day trek.

Marangu Day Trek

Explore lush forests and spot unique wildlife along the "Coca-Cola Route."

Rongai Day Hike

Ideal for a shorter, less crowded adventure with spectacular northern views.

Wildlife & Safari Experiences

Arusha National Park Day Safari

Witness elephants, giraffes, zebras, and more in this scenic park just a short drive from Moshi.

Ngare Sero Waterfall & Wildlife Tour

Hike to stunning waterfalls while spotting local wildlife in a serene forest setting.

Cultural & Village Tours

Moshi Coffee Plantation Tour

Learn about Tanzania's famous coffee, from bean to cup, and taste fresh local brews.

Chagga Village Cultural Experience

Discover traditional life, local cuisine, and the rich history of the Chagga people.

Adventure & Outdoor Activities

Trekking & Nature Trails

Explore scenic trails around Moshi suitable for all fitness levels.

Bike Tours & Mountain Biking

Ride through lush landscapes and enjoy views of Kilimanjaro.

River Rafting & Water Adventures

Thrilling rapids and refreshing waters await on nearby rivers.

Lake Chala & Kikuletwa Hot Springs Tour

Discover the serene beauty and natural wonders of northern Tanzania with our Lake Chala and Kikuletwa Hot Springs Tour. Perfect for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to escape into tranquil landscapes.

Tour Highlights

Lake Chala – Breathtaking crater lake with crystal-clear waters
Kikuletwa Hot Springs – Natural thermal waters surrounded by lush greenery
Scenic Landscapes – Picturesque villages, farmland, and highlands
Cultural Encounters – Interact with local communities

Tour Activities

Guided nature walks Birdwatching & photography Swimming & relaxation Cultural village visits
Full-day tour: 8–10 hours

Pickup and drop-off available from Moshi, Arusha, or nearby towns

Experience two of northern Tanzania's hidden natural wonders in a single day. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a mix of both, the Lake Chala and Kikuletwa Hot Springs Tour offers a memorable escape from the ordinary.
