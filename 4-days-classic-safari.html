4 DAYS CLASSIC SAFARI

Tarangire · Serengeti · Ngorongoro

1

Day 1: Arrival and Tarangire National Park

Morning:

Arrival in Arusha or Kilimanjaro Airport.
Transfer to Tarangire National Park (approx. 2-hour drive).

Midday & Afternoon:

Game drive in Tarangire.
Famous for large herds of elephants, baobab trees, and diverse wildlife.

Evening:

Check-in at Tarangire River Camp or Swala Camp.

Inclusions:

  • • Park fees
  • • Guided game drives
  • • Accommodation
  • • Meals (lunch and dinner)
  • • Drinking water

Exclusions:

  • • Alcoholic beverages
  • • Personal expenses
  • • Tips
  • • Flights
2

Day 2: Tarangire to Serengeti

Morning:

Early breakfast.
Drive to Serengeti National Park (approx. 4-5 hours).

Midday & Afternoon:

Arrive in Serengeti.
Afternoon game drive to see the Big Five and the vast plains.

Evening:

Check-in at Serengeti Sopa Lodge or Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge.

Inclusions:

  • • Park entry fees
  • • Game drives
  • • Accommodation
  • • Meals
  • • Water

Exclusions:

  • • Alcoholic beverages
  • • Personal expenses
  • • Tips
3

Day 3: Full Day Serengeti & Ngorongoro

Morning:

Early morning game drive.
Optional hot air balloon ride (additional cost).

Midday:

Return for breakfast and relax.

Afternoon:

Drive towards Ngorongoro Conservation Area (approx. 2-3 hours).
En route, stop at Olduvai Gorge for a quick visit.

Evening:

Lodge near Ngorongoro, e.g., Ngorongoro Sopa Lodge or Ngorongoro Crater Lodge.

Inclusions:

  • • Park fees
  • • Game drives
  • • Accommodation
  • • Meals
  • • Water

Exclusions:

  • • Hot air balloon ride (optional & extra)
  • • Personal expenses
  • • Tips
4

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater & Return

Morning:

Early breakfast.
Descend into Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive (full day).

Midday & Afternoon:

Picnic lunch on the crater floor.
Wildlife viewing: rhinos, lions, elephants, flamingos, and more.

Late Afternoon:

Drive back to Arusha or onwards.

Inclusions:

  • • Crater fees
  • • Game drives
  • • Picnic lunch
  • • Guide
  • • Transport

Exclusions:

  • • Additional activities
  • • Tips
  • • Items of personal nature

📋 Additional Information

✅ What's Included:

  • • Accommodation at specified lodges/tented camps
  • • Guided game drives
  • • Park entrance fees
  • • Meals and bottled water (as specified)
  • • Transportation within the parks

❌ What's Excluded:

  • • International and domestic flights
  • • Visa fees
  • • Alcoholic beverages and soft drinks (unless included)
  • • Personal expenses
  • • Tips for guides and staff
  • • Hot air balloon safaris (optional)
